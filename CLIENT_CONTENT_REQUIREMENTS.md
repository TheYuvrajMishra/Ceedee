# Client Content Requirements Documentation

## Overview
This document provides detailed specifications for all content and assets required from clients to replace dummy data in the Ceedee project. Each section includes word count guidelines, image specifications, and format requirements.

---

## 1. Company Information

### Basic Details
- **Company Name**: Official registered name
- **Tagline/Slogan**: 5-10 words
- **Founded Year**: Year established
- **Registration Details**: Company registration number, tax ID (if applicable)

### Contact Information
- **Physical Address**: Complete address with postal code
- **Phone Numbers**: Primary, secondary, toll-free (if available)
- **Email Addresses**: General inquiries, support, sales
- **Website URL**: Official website (if different from this project)
- **Social Media Links**: Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube (provide all active profiles)

### About Us Content
- **Company Overview**: 150-250 words describing the company's core business
- **Mission Statement**: 30-50 words
- **Vision Statement**: 30-50 words
- **Core Values**: 3-5 values with brief descriptions (20-30 words each)
- **Company History/Timeline**: 200-400 words or structured timeline format
- **Achievements/Milestones**: 100-200 words or bullet points

### Leadership Team
For each key personnel member:
- **Name**: Full name
- **Position/Title**: Official role
- **Bio**: 80-150 words
- **Photo**: High-resolution headshot (minimum 800x800px, preferably 1200x1200px, PNG or JPG)
- **LinkedIn Profile** (optional)

**Recommended number of team members to feature**: 4-8 key executives

### Media Assets
- **Company Logo**: 
  - Primary logo (full color): SVG preferred, PNG with transparent background (minimum 1000px width)
  - Logo variations: White version, dark version, icon-only version
- **Brand Colors**: Hex codes for primary and secondary colors
- **Company Photos**:
  - Office/facility exterior: 2-3 images (minimum 1920x1080px)
  - Office interior/workspace: 3-5 images
  - Team photos: 2-3 group photos
  - All images should be high-resolution JPG or PNG (minimum 1920x1080px)

## 2. Services & Products

### For Each Service/Product
- **Title**: Service/product name (3-7 words)
- **Short Description**: 30-50 words for preview cards
- **Detailed Description**: 200-400 words explaining features, benefits, and applications
- **Key Features**: 4-8 bullet points (10-20 words each)
- **Specifications/Technical Details**: As detailed as needed (100-300 words or structured table format)
- **Pricing Information**: Starting price, pricing tiers, or "Contact for quote"
- **Use Cases/Applications**: 2-4 examples (30-50 words each)

### Media Assets per Service/Product
- **Hero/Banner Image**: 1 high-resolution image (minimum 1920x1080px)
- **Product/Service Images**: 3-6 supporting images (minimum 1200x800px)
- **Icons**: Custom icons if available (SVG, 512x512px minimum)
- **Brochures/PDFs**: Downloadable resources if available
- **Videos**: Product demos or explainer videos (optional, MP4 format, under 50MB)

**Total number of services/products**: Please specify how many services or products you want to showcase (recommended: 4-12)

---

## 3. News & Events

### News Articles
For each article:
- **Headline**: 5-12 words
- **Summary/Excerpt**: 50-80 words
- **Full Article Content**: 300-800 words
- **Publication Date**: Date of publication
- **Author Name**: Writer or "Company Name Team"
- **Category/Tags**: 2-4 relevant tags
- **Featured Image**: 1 high-resolution image (minimum 1200x630px, ideal for social sharing)
- **Additional Images**: 0-3 supporting images within article content

**Recommended number of news articles**: 6-12 articles (for a healthy news section)

### Events
For each event:
- **Event Title**: 5-10 words
- **Event Description**: 150-300 words
- **Date & Time**: Start and end date/time
- **Location**: Venue name, address, or "Virtual Event"
- **Event Type**: Conference, webinar, trade show, company celebration, etc.
- **Registration Link/Instructions**: URL or contact details
- **Event Image/Banner**: 1 high-resolution image (minimum 1920x1080px)
- **Gallery Images**: 4-8 images if past event (minimum 1200x800px each)

**Recommended number of events**: 3-6 upcoming events + 6-12 past events with photos

## 4. Careers

### Career Page Content
- **Careers Overview**: 150-250 words about company culture and why work here
- **Benefits & Perks**: List of 6-12 benefits with brief descriptions (15-30 words each)
- **Work Environment Description**: 100-150 words
- **Employee Testimonials**: 2-4 testimonials (50-80 words each) with employee names and positions

### Job Listings
For each open position:
- **Job Title**: Official position name
- **Department**: Team or department name
- **Location**: City, state/country or "Remote"
- **Employment Type**: Full-time, part-time, contract, internship
- **Job Summary**: 50-100 words
- **Responsibilities**: 5-10 bullet points (15-30 words each)
- **Requirements/Qualifications**: 5-10 bullet points
- **Preferred Qualifications** (optional): 3-5 bullet points
- **Salary Range** (optional): Salary band or "Competitive"
- **Application Instructions**: How to apply, deadline (if any)

**Current number of open positions**: Please specify

### Career Section Images
- **Office Culture Photos**: 6-10 images showing work environment, team activities (minimum 1200x800px)
- **Team Building/Events Photos**: 4-6 images (minimum 1200x800px)
- **Workplace Facilities**: 3-5 images of amenities, break rooms, etc.

## Content Submission Guidelines

### Format Requirements
- **Text Content**: Submit in Microsoft Word (.docx), Google Docs, or plain text (.txt)
- **Images**: JPG or PNG format, properly named (e.g., "company-logo.png", "service-consulting.jpg")
- **Logos/Icons**: SVG preferred for scalability, or PNG with transparent background
- **Videos**: MP4 format, H.264 codec, under 50MB (or provide YouTube/Vimeo links)
- **Documents/PDFs**: Optimized PDFs under 5MB each

### File Organization
Please organize content in folders:
```
Content_Delivery/
├── 01_Company_Info/
├── 02_Services_Products/
├── 03_News_Events/
├── 04_Careers/
├── 05_CSR/
├── 06_Testimonials_Partners/
├── 07_Legal_Documents/
├── 08_Hero_Landing/
└── 09_Additional_Assets/
```

### Image Quality Standards
- **Minimum Resolution**: 1920x1080px for banners/heroes, 1200x800px for content images
- **File Size**: Optimize images to under 500KB each while maintaining quality
- **Aspect Ratios**: 
  - Hero images: 16:9
  - Product/service images: 3:2 or 4:3
  - Profile photos: 1:1 (square)
  - Social sharing: 1.91:1 (1200x630px)

### Content Style Guidelines
- **Tone**: Professional, clear, and aligned with brand voice
- **Language**: Specify preferred language(s)
- **Writing Style**: Third person for company descriptions, avoid jargon unless industry-specific
- **Call-to-Actions**: Active, engaging verbs

5. **Revisions**: Up to 2 rounds of revisions included

7. **Launch**: Website goes live with approved content
